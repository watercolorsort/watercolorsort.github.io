Fireboy and Watergirl 6: Fairy Tales

Fullscreen

Description

Welcome to the enchanting world of Fireboy and Watergirl 6: Fairy Tales, a captivating adventure game designed for play in your web browser. Join Fireboy and Watergirl on a magical journey through fairy tales, where they'll encounter new challenges, mysteries, and, of course, fairies!

Embark on a Fairy Tale Adventure

In Fireboy and Watergirl 6: Fairy Tales, players will immerse themselves in a world of magic and wonder. Join our elemental heroes as they explore enchanting landscapes filled with gems, obstacles, and magical fairies. Prepare to embark on an unforgettable journey through the realms of fairy tales.

Master the Gameplay

Similar to previous installments, this game requires players to control both Fireboy and Watergirl simultaneously. Use the Arrow Keys to guide Fireboy and the W, A, and D keys to maneuver Watergirl through each level. Remember, they can touch their own elements but must avoid touching the opposite and the green goo.

Utilize Magical Fairies

In this version, players will encounter magic fairies scattered throughout every level. Click and drag your mouse to move them around. These fairies have the power to turn on lamps of their color and activate levers. Keep an eye out for the extra-rare white fairies, as they can turn on lamps of any color. Team up with the fairies to collect gems and overcome obstacles.

Join Forces with Fireboy, Watergirl, and Fairies

Help Fireboy and Watergirl team up with the fairies to collect all the gems and reach the exits! Work together to solve puzzles, activate switches, and navigate through each level. With teamwork and strategy, you can conquer the challenges that await in the world of fairy tales.

How to Play

Use the Arrow Keys to move Fireboy and the W, A, D keys to move Watergirl. They can each touch their own element, but must avoid touching the opposite and the green goo. Click and drag your mouse to move the magic fairies around. Use their powers to turn on lamps and activate levers. Join forces with Fireboy, Watergirl, and the fairies to collect all the gems and reach the exits!
